Montefiore Square Park Redesign

Harlem community park improvement proposal

Manhattan Mahnoor Fatima & Shamor Johnson

Montefiore Square is located at Broadway, Hamilton Place, and West 138th Street. This redesign improves safety, greenery, usability, and community identity.

About the Park

  • Open lawn with terraced seating
  • Garden with perennial plants
  • Plaza for events and markets

Users & Needs

Users

  • Residents need shade + seating
  • Commuters need fast paths
  • Businesses need foot traffic
  • Visitors need direction

Problems

  • Safety conflicts
  • Limited seating
  • Poor lighting
  • Low activity

Before & After

Before

  • Not enough greenery
  • Too much pavement
  • Limited seating
  • Low activity

After

  • Added greenery
  • More seating
  • Better lighting
  • Active space
