A redesigned neighborhood gathering place

This community park in Harlem features an open lawn with terraced stone seating, a garden with perennial plants, and a paved plaza for art installations, markets, festivals, and performances.

Community Care

The Montefiore Park Neighborhood Association assists NYC Parks in maintaining this sloping triangle.

2021 Redesign

The Association played an important role in advocating for the 2021 park redesign.

Events & Gardening

The Association coordinates gardening, facilitates community involvement, and organizes events.
