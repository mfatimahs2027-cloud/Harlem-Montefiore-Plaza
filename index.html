About Montefiore Square

This community park in Harlem features an open lawn with terraced stone seating, a garden with perennial plants, and a paved plaza for events, markets, and performances.

Before: Existing Conditions

Limited greenery and minimal shaded areas.

Hard surfaces dominate the plaza with little comfort.

Limited seating options and lack of flexible space.

The space lacks activity and inviting features.

After: Proposed Redesign

The area was improved with flowers, benches, and trees, making it look more welcoming.

New trash and recycling bins were added to keep the space clean.

Shade from trees makes the area more comfortable for people.

Stores and shops were added, bringing more life and activity to the space.

Highlights

Montefiore Square supports community events, public gathering, and neighborhood activity.
